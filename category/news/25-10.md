<h4 class="section-title underline">New Positions</h4> 
    
<p align="justify">A three year postdoc position in marine sedaDNA at the Geological Survey of Denmark and Greenland in Copenhagen is inviting applications. Application deadline is <b>15th October</b>.</p>
<p align="justify">This position is part of the large interdisciplinary project <a href="https://www.nordforsk.org/projects/marine-phycotoxins-arctic-emerging-climate-change-risk-phate"><b>PHATE - Marine phycotoxins in the Arctic: an emergine climate change risk</b></a> funded by Nordforsk-Sustainable Development of the Arctic.</p>
<p align="justify">The group is seeking a talented and enthusiast researcher to join their team, and use environmental genomics applied to marine sediment cores from the Arctic region to decipher past dynamics of potentially toxic phytoplankton species. The application must include a CV, incl. your ORCID, a list of publications with a separate indication of articles registered in ISI Web of Science and any copies of individual publications of particular relevance to the position. See <a href="https://candidate.hr-manager.net/ApplicationInit.aspx?cid=5001&ProjectId=190425&DepartmentId=6149&MediaId=2436&SkipAdvertisement=False&uiculture=en"><b>link</b></a> for full job description.</p>

 If you have any questions, please contact <a href="mailto:sri@geus.dk"><b>Prof. Sofia Ribeiro</b></a> </p>
